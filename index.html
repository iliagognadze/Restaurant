Looking for somewhere to stay?

Each room offers unique features and timeless design in a first-rate location on the South Bank of the River Thames. From our standard rooms to our river view apartment suites, guests enjoy comfort, luxury and impeccable service throughout their stay.

Accommodation

In addition to breathtaking water views, T Hotel offers a variety of contemporary accommodations sure to make any guest feel right at home like premium satellite channels, free Wi-Fi, minibar, and direct-dial telephone. What’s more, bonus provisions including blackout curtains, sofa bed, shower/tub combination, bidet, and cosy slippers facilitates comfort and convenience. Excellent particularly during hot summer months, air conditioning is provided in all of the guestrooms.
